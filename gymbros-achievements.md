# Gymbros — Achievements & Unlocks

A starter achievement system built around two currencies and a rust-not-strip streak model. Designed so a newer lifter stays neck-and-neck on cosmetics while the stronger lifter still gets bragging rights on raw numbers.

---

## The two currencies

- **IRON** — earned from *absolute* output: total weight moved, max lifts, heavy milestones. The stronger lifter wins here.
- **GRIT** — earned from *behavior*: showing up, streaks, PRs, comebacks, variety. Anyone can win here, and beginners rack it up fast.

Cosmetics are bought with **GRIT**. The flashiest hardcore-only items are bought with **IRON**. Most achievements pay out a fixed chunk of one currency *and* unlock a specific item.

---

## Streak & rust rules (recap)

- **3 rest tokens / month**, refill on the 1st. A missed day auto-spends a token and the streak survives.
- Out of tokens + missed day → **equipped gear goes rusty** (kept, just dull/cracked).
- **2–3 sessions back** → gear shines again, streak resumes from where it rusted (not zero).
- Rust only affects the **equipped set**, never the whole locker.

## Cosmetic categories to fill

- **Fits:** tops, shorts, shoes (common → legendary tiers)
- **Accessories:** belts, chalk, straps, wristbands, headbands
- **Effects:** auras, sweat/ember/lightning accents, animated streak glows
- **Titles & banners:** earned text under the avatar name
- **Emotes/poses:** flex, sweat-drip, deadlift-stance
- **Locker/profile themes:** background skins
- **Joke unlocks:** chicken legs, novelty mankini, etc. — cheap GRIT, high morale

---

## Build order suggestion

1. Currencies + session logging (everything hangs off this)
2. PR detection + Consistency/PR achievements (fastest dopamine, hooks beginners)
3. Rust/rest-token streak system
4. Cosmetics + equip screen
5. Bro/social achievements (needs two accounts wired together)
6. Strength milestones + IRON shop
